About us

Weyland-Yutani Corporation as you know it now was founded in 2099 by the merger of Weyland Corp and the Yutani Corporation. We are searching just for you! We focus on research of Deep space transport, Synthetic manufacture, Extrasolar colonization and Terraforming. Help us explore the unknown space and foreign planets on our newest interstellar cruiser, the Nostromo!

Nostromo

Nostromo is our newest commercial towing vehicle is the Nostromo. It is based on the Lockmart CM-88B Bison, but it is much safer, reliable and so much stronger! Its thrusters can produce powers up to 131 660 kN! We spent over $42 000 000 on the most modern technologies, especially your safety!
