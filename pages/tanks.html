World War II

As you may know, WWII lasted from 1939 to 1945 and the main powers there were the Soviet Union, Nazi Germany, and later on the United States of America. The casualties were enormous, from 70 to 85 million. I'm sorry, but I'm not gonna tell you everything about WWII, gonna talk about tanks that were designed and used during WWII. I'm gonna talk about the most famous tanks from the Soviet Union, Nazi Germany, Great Britain, and the USA.
You can check Wikipedia for more information.

Soviet tanks

For me is one of the most known soviet tanks, the medium tank T-34 and its later modifications like the T-34/85. It was first deployed against Operation Barbarossa. The first version of the T-34 had the 76.2 mm gun and 15 - 45 mm 60° sloped armor which was very effective. Later on, the tank has got a better armor, new turret and the 85 mm gun - the T-34/85. Armour was increased to 20 - 90 mm. The latest version of this tank was the T-44 which had a new hull, turret, same gun as the T-34/85 but increased armor up to 120 mm. The total number built is around 84,070 pieces. Another well-known tank, the heavy one, is the Kliment Voroshilov aka KV which was built around 148 pieces.

German tanks

The Pz. V Panther was a German medium tank that was created to counter Soviet T-34s and replace the Pz. III and Pz. IV. Its 1st introduction was during the Battle of Kursk, which was one of the largest tank battle in history. Panther was equipped with a 75 mm main gun with 79 shells and 16 - 100 mm thick armor. It was built around about 6 000 pieces. The next well-known tank is the Tiger I, a German heavy tank equipped with an 88 mm main gun with 92 shells and armor with thickness from 25 up to 120 mm. The "Tiger" nickname was given by Ferdinand Porsche. 1 347 pieces built.

British tanks

The Cromwell tank was the 1st tank in service combining high speed and reasonable armor. Its 1st action was in the Battle of Normandy, June 1944. Cromwell had a 75 mm main gun with 64 rounds, and 76 - 100 mm armor (depends on version). About 4016 built. The next used tank is the Churchill which was a heavy infantry tank. It had good armor, longitudinal chassis, ability to climb steep slopes. It was one of the heaviest Ally tank. The armor was 102 mm frontally for series I - VI and 152 mm frontally for series VII and VIII. The guns were a 40 mm for series 1 - 2, 57 mm for III - IV series, 75 mm for VI and VII, and 95 mm for V and VIII series.

American tanks

M18 Hellcat was the fastest armored fighting vehicle used by the USA, which was caused by minimal armor and the innovative automatic transition. It was the most effective US TD with the biggest kill-to-loss ratio than any other US TD in WWII. The armor was from 4.8 to 25.4 mm thick and the max speed was 97 km/h on-road and about half off-road. The main gun was the 76 mm gun from M4 Sherman. About 2507 pieces were built. The M4 Sherman, the most widely used MT by the US in WWII. It was reliable and cheap to make. The armor was from 12.7 up to 177.8 mm thick. Shermans were equipped with 3 different main guns from 75 mm to 105 mm. About 49 239 pieces were built excluding prototypes.
